Milli & Co.

Venture Type: Home

The Problem:


According to the Economist, the student housing market is worth US$16 billion worldwide. First time student renters need to find a desirable place to live, however, they have difficulties doing so due to the time consuming process and lack of information.

Target User:


• First time student renters

The Solution:


Milli & Co. is a digital platform that provides first-time renters with easy access to actionable information on neighbourhoods based on the school they attend. The user-focused design of our solution will allow students to save time, lower their stress, and feel confident & informed about the decision making process. Students will no longer need to have dozens of tabs open as we provide all of the relevant information in one place.

Key Features:
• Search based on school you will be attending
• Easily learn about the neighbourhood
• Find out the perks of that area including transit options, proximity to restaurants, safety, and overall desirability
• See the neighbourhood in an interactive map view

Future Opportunities:
• Creation of a browser add-on to instantly provide neighbourhood-related informaton on third-party real estate websites. (Like the Honey coupon browser add-on, but for real estate)

My Role


In a team of four, my role involved researching the total addressable market as well as conducting customer interviews to determine pain-points and emotions that occur with existing solutions.

In addition, I was also responsible for designing the landing page (seen in the top of this webpage) as well as the MVP to demonstrate the proof of concept.
